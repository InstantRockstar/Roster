He has appeared in 141 NHL games during his career, notching 26 goals and adding 32 assists along with 73 penalty minutes. The 22 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of Canada, Jay Bouwmeester was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2002 entry draft. He has appeared in 1072 NHL games during his career, notching 85 goals and adding 305 assists along with 545 penalty minutes. The 34 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of USA, Tage Thompson was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 20 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.

A native of Russia, Klim Kostin was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 19 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of Canada, Brayden McNabb was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 3rd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 271 NHL games during his career, notching 7 goals and adding 66 assists along with 199 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.

A native of Canada, Marc-Andre Fleury was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft.
